A new page in my life

Hello everyone and welcome to my website. Let me introduce myself real quick. I am currently 25 years old, and I have lived all my life in a little town called Leerbeek. It's a very quiet and rural commune with about 1100 inhabitants which is part of the 'Pearl of the Pajottenland'. If you like walking through nature, I can definitely recommend this region!

I have always been very sportive, and I still am. From when I was a kid I played football until I went to University. To have a better balance between school and sport, I started with fitness while maintaining to run a few times a week. When its winter I also like to ride my mountain bike.

Recently I graduated from the Free University of Brussels with a master's degree in clinical psychology. During my studies, I did an eight-month internship at the University Psychiatric Centre KU Leuven, where I worked in the alcohol and drug addiction department. My work consisted of two parts. On the one hand, I did individual and group interviews, on the other hand, I did the diagnostics. I learned a lot about life and how to deal with difficult periods thanks to this internship. But it was when my internship almost came to an end, I felt it wasn't the right direction for me anymore.

So that was the moment I knew I had to make a switch before I even started my career. This is why switchfully was a great opportunity for me to change starting career. So soon I will become a functional analyst!

You are probably wondering what added value someone with a degree in psychology has for this job. My education has given me strong analytical and communicative qualities, and these are exactly the two building blocks for this job!
